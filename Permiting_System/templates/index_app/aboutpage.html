Our Mission

We aim to streamline the livestock transportation permit process in Uganda through a digitized system that enhances efficiency, accuracy, and regulatory compliance.

How Our System Works

Our Digitized Livestock Transportation Permit Verification System simplifies the process of obtaining and verifying permits for livestock movement. Here's how it works:

  1. Permit Application: Users can apply for livestock transportation permits online by providing necessary details such as animal information, transport route, and vehicle details.
  2. Review and Approval: Veterinary officers review the application, inspect the animals if necessary, and approve the permit.
  3. Permit Issuance: Once approved, a digital permit is issued. The permit includes crucial details like transportation route and disease prevention measures.
  4. Verification: Law enforcement and regulatory officers can verify the permit in real-time using a mobile application that scans unique identifiers or QR codes.

Benefits

  • Improved efficiency in permit processing and issuance.
  • Enhanced data accuracy and reduced risk of fraud.
  • Better regulatory oversight and disease control.
  • Convenience for livestock owners and transporters.
